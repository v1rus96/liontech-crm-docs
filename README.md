# LionTech CRM Platform

Welcome to the LionTech CRM Platform documentation.

## Overview

LionTech CRM is a full-featured Customer Relationship Management platform for sales teams. It provides:

- **Contacts** — manage all your leads, prospects, and customers
- **Deal Pipeline** — visual Kanban board for tracking deals through stages
- **Activities** — log calls, emails, meetings, notes, and tasks
- **Dashboard** — real-time KPIs and pipeline analytics
- **Team Management** — role-based access control for your entire team
